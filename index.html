About

A craftsman's approach to every commission.

Fox Wood of Edinburgh is a small, independent woodworking workshop serving homes and clients across the city. Every piece is made with genuine care for the material chosen for its character, worked with hand tools and patience, and built to last for generations.

Whether it's a single statement piece of furniture, a one-of-a-kind handmade gift, or a straightforward carpentry job done properly, the same principle applies: honest work, done well.
